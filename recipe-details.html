Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella slices and a sprinkle of dried oregano.
  5. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle with a little olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot!
